Detailed Description

Class with an exponential atmosphere. The user has to initialize this class with a scale altitude, a density at zero meters altitude and a constant temperature. The density is determined by , with the density at zero altitude, the altitude, and the scaling altitude. The temperature is taken as constant and the pressure follows from the universal gas law .
